How to Pass ATS Scans in 2025

Published on July 14, 2025

Many companies now use ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) to filter job applications. If your resume isn’t optimized for these systems, it may never be seen by a human.

Here are a few tips to ensure your resume is ATS-friendly:

  • ✅ Use a clean layout with no tables or text boxes
  • ✅ Use standard fonts like Arial, Calibri, or Times New Roman
  • ✅ Save your resume as a plain `.pdf` or `.docx` file
  • ✅ Avoid using images or icons in the main text
  • ✅ Include relevant keywords from the job description

Using a builder like SmartCV ensures your resume passes all these checks by default.
